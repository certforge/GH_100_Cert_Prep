# Domain 1 Cheatsheet

- Check `status.github.com` before opening a platform-incident ticket.
- Know support tiers: Community, Standard, Premium, Premium Plus.
- Premium Plus includes a TAM.
- Audit log retention is limited in UI/API; long-term retention needs streaming.
- Enterprise reporting: license usage, Actions usage, and security summaries.
- Track roadmap, changelog, deprecations, and stakeholder communications.
